Welcome to my about page, here you will find more information about myself and my work.

## Background

I've been making games and programming ever since I was a child. I've used quite a few game engines in my time but these I mostly use Unreal Engine 5 and Unity depending on the project. I like to also tinker around and make little tech demos with no engine as well and use a framework like MonoGame, Raylib, or SDL. Sometimes I've even just made games (and a little engine!) that run in the command-line.

Outside of game development, I also write tools and the like in WPF such as the launcher for Day of Despair, a level editor for a custom engine, and modding tools for Garry's Mod.

I've done both singleplayer and multiplayer game development, with the latter being done entirely within Unreal Engine thanks to it's dead simple replication system. That being said, the simplicity of singleplayer development is often preferred over the headache of having to reason about where you code/data is going to be run/stored.

## Work Experience

Most of my experience comes from running my indie game studio but I have also done contract work for other indie studios.

One such contract job was at LumberJack Studios as a Programmer. We used both Unreal and Unity and I did gameplay programming for both and the occasional tool for Unreal. I also did some help with their IT issues since I have experience with that as well.
